Welcome

We're a cosy little coffee shop founded in Bradshaw in 2021 by local barista and coffee aficionado Louisa. We aim to provide a welcoming and inclusive space for you to relax, work (we have free wi-fi!) and socialise over excellent brews, cakes and bakes.

Customer Favourites

Caffe Latte

One or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top.

Carrot Cake

Home baked carrot cake covered in toasted nuts and cream cheese icing. Yum!

Cappuccino

Perfect balance of espresso, steamed milk and foam. Optional chocolate sprinkles.
